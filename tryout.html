Cashflow oracle, yield distribution.

DEX, LP incentives, collateral.

  • DEX liquidity expansion + LP incentives program

  • Additional RWA pools + diversified yield sources

DAO, compliance, cross-chain.

  • DAO governance launch: proposals, voting, treasury controls
  • Compliance framework (jurisdictional rails, reporting, KYC options – dacă e cazul)
  • Cross-chain expansion (bridge + multi-chain liquidity strategy)
  • Institutional onboarding flow + partner integrations
